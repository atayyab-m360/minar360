General

What is Minar360?

Minar360 is a community platform connecting mosques, nonprofits, Muslim-owned businesses, halal restaurants, and community members in one place — so events, prayer times, announcements, and donations don't live in a dozen scattered apps and group chats.

Is Minar360 available in my city yet?

We're onboarding founding organizations city by city rather than launching everywhere at once. Join the founding partner list on the homepage and we'll notify you as soon as your area opens up.

Is there a mobile app?

The mobile app is currently in development. In the meantime, the website gives you a preview of how event discovery, prayer times, and organization profiles will work.

How is Minar360 different from Luma, Eventbrite, or Zeffy?

General event platforms handle a single ticket link well, but weren't built around a faith community. Minar360 combines event discovery with mosque directories, prayer times, and halal business listings — all in one feed, instead of scattered across separate tools.

For Community Members

Is Minar360 free to use?

Yes. Browsing events, mosque directories, prayer times, and business listings is completely free for community members.

How do I find events near me?

Use the search bar on the homepage to look up events, mosques, or halal spots by name or location. As more organizations join, your local feed will fill in automatically based on where you are.

Can I get prayer time notifications?

Yes — the app will support prayer time and athan notifications. You can preview how the athan alert works in the demo widget on the homepage.

How do I join a mosque or organization's group?

Once an organization is onboarded, their profile includes a way to follow or join their group directly, so their events and announcements show up in your feed automatically.

For Organizations & Nonprofits

Is it free for mosques and nonprofits to join?

Creating a profile and publishing events, prayer times, and announcements is free. Optional paid tools are available for organizations that want more advanced features.

Can we accept donations through Minar360?

Yes, donation collection is one of the core organization tools. Processing is handled through standard, secure payment providers rather than anything custom-built, so your organization's funds are handled the same way any reputable payment processor handles them.

Can we sell event tickets?

Yes. Organizations can create ticketed or free events, with RSVPs and attendance tracked in one place.

How do we publish prayer times and announcements?

Once your organization profile is set up, prayer times and announcements are published from your dashboard and appear automatically on your public profile and in your followers' feeds.

Can multiple staff members manage our organization's profile?

Yes, organizations will be able to add multiple team members with their own logins to manage events, donations, and announcements together.

For Businesses & Restaurants

Can my business or restaurant get listed?

Yes. Muslim-owned businesses and halal restaurants have their own profile type built for discovery, separate from event listings.

Do you verify that a business is Muslim-owned or halal?

We're still finalizing our verification process as we onboard founding businesses. Details will be shared directly with businesses as they join.

Is there a fee for business listings?

Founding businesses are being onboarded with special early terms. Reach out through the homepage to learn about current pricing.

Prayer Times & Athan

How accurate are the prayer times?

The times shown in the current homepage demo are sample placeholders for preview purposes. In the live app, prayer times will be calculated based on your actual location.

Can I get an athan notification?

Yes — you'll be able to enable athan alerts for each prayer time. You can try a preview of this in the demo widget on the homepage.

Which calculation method do you use?

We plan to support multiple standard calculation methods (such as ISNA and the Muslim World League) so organizations and individuals can choose the convention their community follows.

Donations & Payments

What payment processors do you support?

Donations and payments are handled through established, secure payment providers rather than anything custom-built.

Are there transaction fees?

Standard payment processing fees apply, similar to any donation or ticketing platform. Specific rates will be shared clearly with organizations before they go live.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

That depends entirely on the tax-exempt status of the organization you're donating to, not on Minar360 itself. Check with the receiving organization or a tax professional to confirm their status before assuming a donation is deductible.

Privacy & Data

Is my personal information sold or shared?

No. Personal data is not sold to third parties.

How is my donation and payment information protected?

Payment details are handled directly by the payment processor, following industry-standard encryption and security practices, rather than being stored on Minar360's own servers.

Can I control what notifications I receive?

Yes, notification preferences — including prayer alerts, event reminders, and organization updates — will be adjustable from your account settings.

Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Is Minar360 free for mosques and nonprofits?

Yes — creating a profile and publishing events, prayer times, and announcements is free. Optional paid tools are available for organizations that want more.

How is this different from just posting on Instagram or a group chat?

Instagram posts and group chats disappear in the feed. Minar360 keeps your events, prayer times, and profile in a place your community can search and find later.

Can businesses and restaurants join without being a mosque or nonprofit?

Yes. Muslim-owned businesses and halal restaurants have their own profile type built for discovery, not events.

When can I download the app?

The app is currently in development. Join the founding partner list above and we'll let you know as soon as it's available.
